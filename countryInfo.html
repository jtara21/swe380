What you should know about Senegal.

Where is Senegal and How is the weather there?

Senegal is located at the far west of the african continent. It is bordered by the Atlantic Ocean to the west, Mauritania to the north-northeast, Mali to the east, Guinea to the southeast, and Guinea-Bissau to the south-southwest. Senegal is located in the intertropical zone, experiencing a hot and sunny climate. The dry season extends from October to June and the rainy season from July to September. From north to south, the climate changes from Sahelian to Sudanian.

The country and the population

The capital of Senegal is Dakar. The country has 17 million inhabitants. The official language of Senegal is French and the national language Wolof. Islam represents the majority religion (90%), the rest of the population being Catholic or animist. The weight of religion is very important in Senegal with sometimes the practice of several religions simultaneously.

Economy and politics

Senegal is one of the best performing economies in sub-Saharan Africa. For several years now, the economy has recorded sustained growth above 6%. Its long history of social and political stability and its ever-improving business environment make it a safe destination for investment. Unlike many African countries, Senegal has never experienced a military coup, civil war, or ethnic or religious conflict, with the exception of a separatist conflict in its southern part in the 1980s. has a long tradition of peace and democracy. So you can feel safe in this country. However, it is necessary to inquire because it is possible to see certain dangerous zones.
